The Founder & Lead Architect

Afrasyaab Meranai

Al-Haq Studio (Al-Haq.uk) was founded and is entirely developed and maintained by Afrasyaab Meranai.

As a sole developer, Afrasyaab's mission is to design high-quality, local-first applications that safeguard digital wellness and help users protect their faith and attention in an era of constant distraction. Every product, from native Kotlin accessibility blocker modules to TensorFlow Lite neural network engines, is built offline-first without third-party tracking, advertising, or data sales.
